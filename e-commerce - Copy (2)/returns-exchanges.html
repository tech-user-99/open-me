Frequently Asked Questions

How long do refunds take to appear in my account?

Refunds typically appear in your account within 5-10 business days after we receive and process your return. Credit card refunds may take an additional 3-5 business days to appear on your statement.

What if I received a damaged or defective item?

Contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos of the damaged item. We'll arrange a free replacement or refund and cover all shipping costs.

Can I exchange for a different color or size?

Yes! Request an exchange when initiating your return. If the replacement item is unavailable, we'll offer a refund or store credit for the full amount.

Do I need to pay for return shipping?

Returns due to our error or defective items include free return shipping. For other returns, a small shipping fee may be deducted from your refund.
