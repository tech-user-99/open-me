Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping, shipping, returns, and more.

Orders & Payments

How do I place an order?

To place an order: 1) Browse products and add items to your cart, 2) Click the cart icon and review your items, 3) Proceed to checkout, 4) Enter shipping details, 5) Select payment method and complete purchase. You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and ShopEasy store credit. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. Contact our support team at support@shopeasy.com or call +1 (555) 123-4567. Once an order has shipped, you'll need to follow our returns process.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping: 3-7 business days. Express shipping: 1-2 business days. International shipping: 7-14 business days. You'll receive tracking information as soon as your order ships.

How do I track my order?

Track your order via: 1) The tracking link in your shipping confirmation email, 2) Your account's order history, or 3) Contact our customer service with your order number.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unworn, unused, in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping is free for defective items. See our full Returns Policy.

How do I start a return?

Start returns through your account or email returns@shopeasy.com with your order number. We'll provide a prepaid return label if eligible.

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days after we receive your return. The refund will appear on your original payment method within 3-5 additional business days.

Products & Sizing

How do I choose the right size?

Check our detailed size guides on each product page. You can also contact our style advisors for personalized sizing recommendations.

Are your products true to size?

Most customers find our products true to size. Check customer reviews for real feedback on fit. If between sizes, we recommend sizing up.

How do I care for my products?

Care instructions are on each product's label. Generally: machine wash cold, tumble dry low, and avoid bleach to maintain quality.

Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions in the reset email. For security, reset links expire in 24 hours.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use 256-bit SSL encryption and never store your full payment details. All transactions comply with PCI DSS security standards.

How do I update my account information?

Log into your account, go to "Account Settings," and update your information. Changes to email require verification for security.
